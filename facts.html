Interesting facts from India's freedom movement

1
India got independence on August 15, 1947, after almost 200 years of British rule.
2
Mahatma Gandhi led the Dandi March in 1930 to protest the British salt tax.
3
The Indian National Congress was formed in 1885, and played a crucial role in the freedom movement.
4
Bhagat Singh was only 23 when he was executed, becoming a national hero for his bravery.
5
India's tricolour flag was first hoisted on August 7, 1906 in Kolkata.
6
Subhas Chandra Bose created the Indian National Army to fight British forces.
7
The Quit India Movement was launched by Gandhi on August 8, 1942, demanding British withdrawal.
8
At the time of independence, India had no national anthem; "Jana Gana Mana" was officially adopted in 1950.
